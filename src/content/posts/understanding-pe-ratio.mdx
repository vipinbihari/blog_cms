# Understanding the Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio

The Price-to-Earnings (P/E) ratio is one of the most widely used valuation metrics in stock market investing. It provides a quick way to determine whether a company's stock price is potentially overvalued or undervalued relative to its earnings.
